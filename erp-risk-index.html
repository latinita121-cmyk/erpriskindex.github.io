How to Read the Index

  • Documented Cases = entries included in this database (not all global deployments).
  • Total Documented Impact = reported costs, write-offs, settlements, or audited uncertainty where documented.
  • Risk Level = a simple band based on magnitude + severity in documented outcomes (not predictive).

This database records documented outcomes only. It does not assert intent, fault, or misconduct beyond cited sources.
