ERP Risk Index

ERP Risk Index documents publicly reported ERP failures, litigation, and government oversight actions to support due diligence by executives, procurement teams, advisors, and auditors.

The database focuses on verifiable outcomes and primary evidence sources, including government audits, court records, and official inquiries.

Current public release: Index v1.0
